The Manhattan Drink

What is the manhattan drink?

The Manhattan drink is a classic in the world of cocktails. A simple yet classy concoction.
The warmimg burn of whiskey misked with the exotic accent of vermouth will tingle the senses of all who try it.

Ingredients

  • 2 ounces rye whiskey
  • 1 ounce sweet vermouth
  • 2 dashes Angostura bitteres
  • 1 dash orange bitteres
  • Garnish: brandied cherry or lemon twist
